Internet Safety Rules

Learn how to stay safe while using the Internet!

1. Keep Personal Info Private

Do not share your full name, address, or passwords online.

2. Use Strong Passwords

Always use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols to create strong passwords.

3. Avoid Clicking Unknown Links

Do not click links from unknown sources; they may contain viruses or phishing attempts.

4. Ask Permission Before Downloading

Always check with a parent or teacher before downloading files from the Internet.

5. Be Kind Online

Respect others and avoid posting hurtful or mean messages.
