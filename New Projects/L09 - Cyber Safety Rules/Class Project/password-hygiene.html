Password Hygiene

Passwords are like the keys to your online accounts. A good password keeps your information safe from people who might try to steal it. Never use easy passwords like “123456” or your name.

Make your password strong by using a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. For example, instead of “sunshine,” you could use “Sun!23Shine.” The stronger the password, the harder it is for someone to guess.

Don’t share your passwords with friends, and change them often. If you think someone knows your password, change it right away. Remember: a strong password is your first defense online!
