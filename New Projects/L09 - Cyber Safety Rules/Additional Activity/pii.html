Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

Home Access Control Mobile Devices Password Hygiene Child Safety

PII means “Personally Identifiable Information.” This is information that tells others who you are, like your full name, address, phone number, school name, or date of birth.

Never share your PII online with people you don’t know. For example, don’t post your address or phone number on social media or in a game chat. Even small details can help strangers find out more about you.

If someone online asks for your PII, don’t answer—tell a parent, teacher, or trusted adult. Protecting your PII helps keep you safe from scams and strangers.
